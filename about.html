About Read Healthy

ReadHealthy was founded by four college students concerned about health literacy throughout the United States, especially in under privileged areas.

The ReadHealthy team seeks to promote health literacy through interactive education, as well as a complete curriculum based on the Center for Disease Control's health literacy standards.
